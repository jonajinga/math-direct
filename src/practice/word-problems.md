**Read each problem and solve.**

1. Sam has 12 apples. He gives away 5. How many are left? **?**

2. There are 8 birds in a tree. 6 more land on a branch. How many birds now? **?**

3. A box has 15 crayons. 7 are broken. How many are not broken? **?**

4. Emma reads 9 pages on Monday and 9 on Tuesday. How many total? **?**

5. There are 20 chairs. 8 are taken. How many are empty? **?**

6. A farmer has 34 eggs. He sells 12. How many are left? **?**

7. One shelf has 25 books. Another has 14. How many books altogether? **?**

8. A class has 17 girls and 16 boys. How many students total? **?**

**Answers:** 1) 7 &nbsp; 2) 14 &nbsp; 3) 8 &nbsp; 4) 18 &nbsp; 5) 12 &nbsp; 6) 22 &nbsp; 7) 39 &nbsp; 8) 33
